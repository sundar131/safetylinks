Welcome to Safety Links!

Inspection Services & Audit

We provide inspection and certification of all equipment in industry under THE FACTORIES ACT 1948 & TN FACTORIES RULES 1950. We provide safety audit as per IS14489-1998 and also customer preferences.

Safety Training

We provide training for Manual and Material handling training, First Aid Training, Fire Fighting and Mock drill training and customer preferred training related to safety.

Safety consultant
